Wonseok Shin is a principal researcher at the **Korea Institute of Industrial Technology (KITECH)**. His research focuses on robotic actuation systems, biomechatronics, and advanced mechatronic technologies for wearable and assistive robotic platforms.

His work explores the design and control of robotic actuators, transmission systems, and mechanical architectures that enable high-performance human–robot interaction.
